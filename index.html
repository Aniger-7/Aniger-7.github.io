Regina Mehta

Hi! My name is Regina. I come from the hills and like most hills' people I love telling stories. I've grown up hearing plenty of them, from mythology to folk to classic hill horror. A few months ago I enrolled in Google Data Analytics Course and yet again my old inclination towards story telling was boosted. With the tools and technology I learned during the course, not only could I interpret data but I could tell stories in the most compelling ways,in data-driven ways. The myriad ways in which the same dataset can be interpreted and the endless insights each datapoint has to offer really excited me. So to put my skills to use and to hon them further I decided to take up projects and showcase them here on my portfolio.

I believe in living how John O'Donohue puts it, like how a river flows, carried by the surprise of its own unfolding. On this portfolio you are welcome to witness my data analytics journey, yet another unfolding.
